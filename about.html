What we do?

The Institute Technical Council (ITC) at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay is the central body responsible for overseeing and promoting technical activities across the institute. Our mission is to empower students by providing opportunities to explore diverse technical domains, collaborate on impactful projects, and develop real-world skills.ITC supports various clubs and teams, organizing workshops, competitions, and events throughout the year.
